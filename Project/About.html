Our Mission

At Anything But Read, our mission is to showcase all the amazing things libraries offer beyond books. We're aware of the ever evolving world and even so we belive that libraries can provide a unique experiance for the commuity. From creative workshops and tech classes to fun events and community programs, we believe libraries are vibrant spaces for learning, creativity, and connection. We’re here to help you explore everything your local library has to offer—whether you're a kid, teen, or adult
