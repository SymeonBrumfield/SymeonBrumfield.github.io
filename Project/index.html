What can your library do for you in addition to providing books?

At Anything But Read, we believe libraries are so much more than just books! Libraries today are public spaces filled with exciting activities and endless possibilities for both kids and adults. For example, did you know that many libraries offer free computer and internet access? Whether you want to browse the web, work on a school project, or learn something new, libraries provide the technology and tools you need. They even have fun tech workshops where you can learn how to code or design your own video games! Below we've provided a list of services libraries provide.

  • Movie Night
  • Yoga Classes
  • Resume Workshops
  • Game Nighs
  • And More!
