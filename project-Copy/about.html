Our Story

In January 2024, Brownsville, Texas, welcomed Barron’s Tacos, a food truck founded by Jonathan and Zeilaliz Barron, who brought their passion for Mexican street food from their homeland. Inspired by joyful family gatherings, they decided to share their culinary heritage with the local community.

Their colorful food truck quickly became a beacon for taco lovers, featuring authentic options like al pastor, barbacoa, and elote, all made from fresh ingredients. On opening day, a lively crowd gathered, drawn by the tantalizing aromas. Jonathan grilled the meats while Zeilaliz prepared homemade salsas, making every customer feel like family.

As word spread, Barron’s Tacos became a local favorite, hosting taco nights and community events that fostered connection and laughter. By the end of the year, their food truck had transformed into a celebration of culture and community, bringing a taste of home to Brownsville and turning their dream into a delicious reality.
